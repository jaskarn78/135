Recursive Descent Parser in PHP!

Please enter an expression derivable from the following grammar and that also ends in the symbol '$'.
EXP ::= EXP + TERM | EXP - TERM | TERM
TERM ::= TERM * FACTOR | TERM / FACTOR | FACTOR
FACTOR ::= ( EXP ) | DIGIT
DIGIT ::= 0 | 1 | 2 | 3
Valid Example: (3+2)-1$
Invalid Example: 5-)-2*7$
